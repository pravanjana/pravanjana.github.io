This is the first post on this site — mostly here to confirm the blog pipeline works end to end.

To publish a new post going forward, add a file to `_posts/` named `YYYY-MM-DD-your-title.md`, with front matter like this at the top:

```
---
layout: post
title: "Your Post Title"
---
```

Then write the post content below the front matter in Markdown. Commit and push, and it shows up on the [Blog page]({{ '/blog.html' | relative_url }}) automatically — no other setup needed.

Feel free to delete this post once you've published something real.
